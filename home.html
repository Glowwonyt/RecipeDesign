Fillet Steak with

roasted vegetables

4 out of 5

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Season the fillet steaks with salt and black pepper.

  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  4. Add the fillet steaks to the skillet and sear them on each side until they are browned, about 3-4 minutes per side.

  5. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes for medium-rare, or until they are cooked to your liking.

  6. While the steaks are cooking, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a separate skillet over medium heat.

  7. Add the garlic, thyme, bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  8. Drizzle the balsamic vinegar over the vegetables and stir to combine.
